Museum Policies

Rules and guidelines for visitors

General Guidelines

Visitors are kindly requested to respect the museum environment by maintaining silence in exhibition halls, avoiding touching artifacts, and following all instructions provided by museum staff to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone.

Photography Policy

Photography is permitted in most areas of the museum for personal use only. However, the use of flash, tripods, or professional equipment may be restricted in certain galleries to protect delicate artifacts and preserve their condition.

Tickets & Entry

All tickets are valid for a single entry on the selected date and time. Visitors are advised to arrive at least 15 minutes earlier to complete security checks and enjoy a smooth entry process without delays.

Security Rules

For the safety of both visitors and collections, all guests must pass through security screening at the entrance. Large bags, sharp objects, and prohibited items are strictly not allowed inside the museum premises.
