☥ THE GRAND EGYPTIAN MUSEUM

The Grand Egyptian Museum is one of the largest archaelogical museums in the world.It showcases thousands of ancient Egyptian artifacts and tells the story of Egypt's rich history

Purpose of the Museum

  • Preserve ancient Egyptian artifacts
  • Present Egypt's history in a modern and inteactive way
  • Support tourism in Egypt
  • Educate visitors about ancient civilizaion

Unique Collection

For the first time ever,the complete collection of Tutankhamun's treasures is displayed together in one place

Museum Area

One of the largest museums in the world, covering a total area of about 480,000 square meters.

Opening

The Grand Egyptian Museum opened in a soft phase, with key galleries accessible and full opening expected in 2025.

Collections

The museum holds over 100,000 artifacts, including the complete collection of Tutankhamun’s treasures.
